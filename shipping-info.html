Shipping Information

We aim to deliver your order as quickly and efficiently as possible. Below you'll find details about our shipping process, delivery times, and charges.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

2. Delivery Timeline

  • India (Standard Shipping): 4–7 business days
  • India (Express Shipping): 2–4 business days
  • International Shipping: 7–14 business days (varies by country)

3. Shipping Charges

  • Standard Shipping: ₹49 (Free on orders above ₹999)
  • Express Shipping: ₹99 flat
  • International: Calculated at checkout based on location

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, a tracking ID and link will be emailed to you. You can also track your order here.

5. Shipping Carriers

We work with trusted carriers like Delhivery, Blue Dart, and India Post for domestic deliveries, and DHL or FedEx for international orders.

6. Delays

While we do our best to ensure timely deliveries, occasional delays may occur due to weather, high demand, or courier service disruptions.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about your order or shipping, feel free to contact us.
