Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our top priority. Please read our policy below to understand your rights regarding returns and refunds.

1. Eligibility for Return

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • The product must be unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging.
  • Returns are not accepted for items on final sale or marked as non-returnable.

2. Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact us at support@otakudrips.com with your order ID and reason for return. Once approved, we’ll share return shipping instructions.

3. Refunds

  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after receiving and inspecting the returned item.
  • The refund will be made to your original payment method.
  • Shipping fees (if any) are non-refundable.

4. Exchanges

We currently do not support exchanges. If you'd like a different item or size, please return the original product and place a new order.

5. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us immediately. We’ll arrange a replacement at no additional cost.

Need Help?

Have a question or concern about your order? Get in touch with us, and we’ll be happy to assist you.
